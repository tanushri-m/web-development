Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp onions
  • Chopped vegetables
    • Carrots
    • Spinach
    • Corrinader
  • Cumin seeds

Method

  1. Put 1 tbsp butter in pan
  2. Allow it to heat for a second
  3. Mix the eggs along with chopped vegetables and stir it for 5 mins, till it is properly mashed
  4. Poue the mixture in the Pan.
  5. Cover the pan with lid and allow it to cooked
  6. Chech and flip to other sides
  7. Dish is ready to be served
